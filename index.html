How we handle uncertainty

Confidence levels

  • Confirmed — supported by strong source evidence
  • Likely — supported, but not perfectly resolved
  • Mixed signals — meaningful source disagreement exists
  • Unable to confirm — evidence is incomplete or weak

Why that matters

Serious buyers do not just need more data. They need disciplined interpretation of what the data actually means and what still needs to be checked before committing.
