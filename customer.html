Questions to ask the seller next

  1. Can you send photos that confirm the claimed options and interior features?
  2. What major maintenance has been done in the last 20k miles?
  3. Do you have receipts or service records for cooling, suspension, brakes, or oil leaks?
  4. Can you send cold-start and walkaround video?
